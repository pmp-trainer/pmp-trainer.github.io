About

Trainer Riad Thalji; one of the best international professional trainers, is the owner of Thalji.net and has a sound experience in this field due to the fact that he is coming from the real-life business.

He is a Previous IT Analysis Manager and PMO Section Chief at Orange Telecom. The Project Management Institute shows his name within the reviewers of their PMBOK Fifth Edition.

Riad is one of the trainers who dedicates the rest of his life for human development through building employment skills and competencies in his different courses that carefully target the business market demands.

Through Thalji.net, he provided PMP and professional training to top companies in the Middle East, North Africa, Malaysia and Paris.

Trainer Riad Works through his Thalji.net, and sometimes through Other Training Institutes in different countries.

Riad is the author of the book:

"A Practical Approach to PMP Exam Preparation"
